About

This is a simple application for pixel art creation.

Basic Controls

These are on the right of the controls toolbar.

Color: change your brush color.

Resize: change the number of rows and number of cols for your picture. Range of allowed rows and columns are each 1-100. Note: command automatically clears the picture.

Intermediate Controls

These are on the left of the controls toolbar, within the dropdowns.

Tools

  Brush: change pixels into your chosen brush color.

  Eraser: change pixels back to white.

Draw Method

  Draw On Click: Use your Brush/Eraser when you click or tap on a block

  Draw On Drag: Use your Brush/Eraser when you drag over a set of blocks. Not compatible with touchscreens. Warning: this can be buggy.

Other

  Toggle Border: Toggle where or not your pixels have black border around them.

  Clear: clear the entire picture.
